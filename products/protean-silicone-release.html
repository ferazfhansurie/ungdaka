Product Description

Protean Silicone Release is a versatile NSF H1 lubricant designed for multiple applications in food processing facilities. This food-safe product provides excellent lubrication and protection for various equipment types while ensuring food safety compliance.

  • NSF H1 registered for food processing applications
  • Release Agent formulation
  • Excellent penetrating properties
  • Good water displacement
  • Corrosion protection
  • Clean, non-staining formula

Applications

  • General machinery lubrication
  • Chains and conveyors
  • Hinges and slides
  • Light-duty bearings
  • Food processing equipment
  • Packaging machinery

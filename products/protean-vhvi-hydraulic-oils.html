Protean VHVI Hydraulic Oils

NSF H1 Food-Safe Very High VI

Product Description

Protean VHVI Hydraulic Oils are premium quality NSF H1 lubricants formulated with Very High Viscosity Index (VHVI) base oils. These advanced hydraulic fluids provide exceptional performance and stability across a wide temperature range while meeting food-safe requirements for incidental food contact.

Key Features

  • NSF H1 registered for food processing
  • Very High Viscosity Index for temperature stability
  • Outstanding wear protection
  • Superior oxidation resistance
  • Excellent water separation
  • Extended drain intervals

Applications

  • Food processing equipment
  • Hydraulic systems
  • Packaging machinery
  • Bottling equipment
  • Cold storage facilities
  • General food manufacturing

Available Grades

  • ISO VG 32
  • ISO VG 46
  • ISO VG 68
  • ISO VG 100

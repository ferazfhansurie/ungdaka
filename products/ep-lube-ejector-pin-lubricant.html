EP Lube Ejector Pin Lubricant

EP Protection Anti-Wear High Load

Product Description

EP Lube is an advanced extreme pressure lubricant specifically designed for ejector pins and sliding mechanisms in plastic injection molding applications. This high-performance formula provides exceptional wear protection, reduces friction, and extends component life under high-load conditions while creating a durable lubricating film that withstands high temperatures and frequent cycling.

Key Features

  • Superior extreme pressure protection
  • Excellent wear resistance
  • High load-carrying capacity
  • Extended component life
  • High temperature stability
  • Reduced friction and wear
  • Clean, non-staining operation

Applications

  • Ejector pins in injection molding
  • Sliding mechanisms
  • Guide pins and bushings
  • Core pins
  • Mold components
  • High-load bearing surfaces
  • Precision mold parts

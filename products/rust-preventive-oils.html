Rust Preventive Oils

Oil Based Solvent Based Long-Term Protection

Product Description

Our Rust Preventive Oils are available in both oil-based and solvent-based formulations, designed to provide superior protection against corrosion for metal parts and equipment. These specialized products create a protective barrier that prevents rust formation during storage, handling, and transportation. The dual offering allows customers to choose the most suitable solution based on their specific application requirements and environmental conditions.

Key Features

  • Available in oil-based and solvent-based formulations
  • Forms a strong protective barrier
  • Long-lasting corrosion protection
  • Excellent surface coverage
  • Easy application and removal
  • Suitable for indoor and outdoor storage
  • Compatible with most metal surfaces

Applications

  • Metal parts and components storage
  • Machinery protection during transport
  • Long-term equipment storage
  • Steel product preservation
  • Manufacturing process protection
  • Marine equipment storage
  • Industrial tool preservation

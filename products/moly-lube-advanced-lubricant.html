Moly Lube Advanced Lubricant

Extreme Pressure High Performance Advanced Formula

Product Description

Moly Lube is an advanced molybdenum-based lubricant specifically formulated for extreme pressure and high-stress applications. This sophisticated formula provides exceptional load-bearing capacity and wear protection while reducing friction in the most demanding industrial environments. The molybdenum disulfide additives ensure superior performance under heavy loads and high temperatures.

Key Features

  • Superior extreme pressure performance
  • Excellent wear protection
  • High temperature stability
  • Reduced friction coefficient
  • Extended equipment life
  • Enhanced load capacity

Applications

  • Heavy-duty machinery
  • High-load bearings
  • Industrial gearboxes
  • Mining equipment
  • Construction machinery
  • Wire ropes and cables
  • Chain drives and conveyors

Benefits

  • Significantly reduces friction and wear
  • Extends equipment service life
  • Lowers maintenance costs
  • Improves operational efficiency
  • Protects against rust and corrosion
  • Reduces energy consumption

Technical Information

  • Base: Advanced molybdenum disulfide formula
  • Color: Dark grey/black
  • Operating Temperature: -30°C to 450°C
  • Application Method: Spray
  • Film Type: Non-drying, protective
  • Load Capacity: Excellent under extreme pressure

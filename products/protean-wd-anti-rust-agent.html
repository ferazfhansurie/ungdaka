Protean WD Anti-Rust Agent

Food Grade Multi-Purpose NSF H1

Product Description

Protean WD Anti-Rust Agent is a food-grade water displacing anti-rust agent providing superior corrosion protection for equipment in food processing environments. This NSF H1 registered product effectively displaces moisture and prevents rust formation while ensuring food safety compliance.

Key Features

  • NSF H1 registered for food processing applications
  • Superior water displacement properties
  • Excellent penetrating capabilities
  • Long-lasting corrosion protection
  • Effective rust prevention
  • Clean, non-staining formula

Applications

  • Metal surface protection
  • Equipment maintenance
  • Moisture displacement
  • Rust prevention on tools and equipment
  • Food processing machinery
  • Corrosion protection during storage

Protean Fast Dry Solvent Cleaner

Food Grade Fast Drying NSF H1

Product Description

Protean Fast Dry Solvent Cleaner is a versatile food grade lubricant designed for multiple applications in food processing facilities. This NSF H1 registered product provides excellent lubrication and protection for various equipment types while ensuring food safety compliance.

Key Features

  • NSF H1 registered for food processing applications
  • Fast Drying formulation
  • Excellent penetrating properties
  • Good water displacement
  • Corrosion protection
  • Clean, non-staining formula

Applications

  • General machinery lubrication
  • Chains and conveyors
  • Hinges and slides
  • Light-duty bearings
  • Food processing equipment
  • Packaging machinery

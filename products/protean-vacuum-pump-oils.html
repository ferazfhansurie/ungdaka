Protean Vacuum Pump Oils

Food Grade NSF H1 High Performance

Product Description

Protean Vacuum Pump Oils are premium quality food grade lubricants specifically designed for vacuum pumps in food processing facilities. These oils provide excellent oxidation stability and wear protection while meeting NSF H1 requirements for incidental food contact.

Key Features

  • NSF H1 registered for food processing applications
  • Outstanding oxidation stability
  • Excellent wear protection
  • Low volatility
  • Extended service life
  • Superior moisture separation

Applications

  • Food packaging equipment
  • Processing vacuum systems
  • Food preservation equipment
  • Vacuum packaging lines
  • General food processing

Available Grades

  • ISO VG 32
  • ISO VG 46
  • ISO VG 68
  • ISO VG 100

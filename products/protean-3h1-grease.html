Protean 3H1 Grease

NSF 3H/H1 Food-Safe Direct Food Contact

Product Description

Protean 3H1 Grease has both NSF 3H and H1 registrations, making it suitable for both direct and incidental food contact applications. This versatile, food-safe grease provides excellent lubrication while meeting the strictest food safety requirements.

Key Features

  • NSF 3H registered for direct food contact
  • NSF H1 registered for incidental food contact
  • Excellent release properties
  • Superior water resistance
  • Clean, tasteless, and odorless
  • FDA 21 CFR 178.3570 compliant

Applications

  • Food processing equipment
  • Release applications
  • Bakery equipment
  • Conveyor systems
  • Packaging machinery
  • Meat and poultry processing

Benefits

  • Safe for direct food contact
  • Reduced contamination risk
  • Extended equipment life
  • Improved food safety compliance
  • Versatile applications

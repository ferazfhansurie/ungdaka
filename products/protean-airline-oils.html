Protean Airline Oils

Food Grade NSF H1 Pneumatic Systems

Product Description

Protean Airline Oils are specialized food grade lubricants designed for pneumatic systems in food processing environments. These oils offer excellent lubrication and protection while meeting NSF H1 requirements for incidental food contact, ensuring safe operation in food processing facilities.

Key Features

  • NSF H1 registered for food industry use
  • Excellent low temperature performance
  • Superior oxidation stability
  • Low deposit formation
  • Excellent water separation
  • Extended service intervals

Applications

  • Pneumatic systems in food plants
  • Air line lubricators
  • Food packaging equipment
  • Processing machinery
  • Air tools in food areas

Available Grades

  • ISO VG 32
  • ISO VG 46
  • ISO VG 68

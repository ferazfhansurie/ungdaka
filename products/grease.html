Grease (NSF H1)

NSF H1 Food Safe Non-Toxic

Product Description

Our NSF H1 grease is specially formulated for use in food processing equipment where incidental food contact may occur. This high-performance grease provides excellent lubrication while meeting strict food safety requirements. It offers superior protection against wear, corrosion, and oxidation while being tasteless, odorless, and physiologically inert.

Key Features

  • NSF H1 registered for incidental food contact
  • Excellent wear protection
  • Superior water resistance
  • Wide temperature range
  • Oxidation resistant
  • Tasteless and odorless
  • Compliant with FDA regulations

Applications

  • Food processing equipment
  • Beverage packaging machinery
  • Pharmaceutical equipment
  • Bottling and canning lines
  • Conveyor systems
  • Mixing equipment
  • Clean room applications

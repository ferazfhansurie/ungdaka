Protean Compressor Oils

NSF H1 Food-Safe NSF H1 High Performance

Product Description

Protean Compressor Oils are premium quality NSF H1 lubricants specifically designed for air compressors in food processing facilities. These oils provide excellent oxidation stability and wear protection while meeting food-safe requirements for incidental food contact.

Key Features

  • NSF H1 registered for food processing applications
  • Outstanding oxidation and thermal stability
  • Excellent wear protection
  • Low carbon formation
  • Extended drain intervals
  • Superior water separation

Applications

  • Rotary screw compressors
  • Reciprocating compressors
  • Vane compressors
  • Food processing facilities
  • Beverage manufacturing

Available Grades

  • ISO VG 32
  • ISO VG 46
  • ISO VG 68
  • ISO VG 100

Our Story

Ungdaka (M) Sdn. Bhd. has been at the forefront of industrial solutions for over two decades. Established with a vision to provide advanced technology solutions to cleanliness and lubrications, we have grown to become a trusted partner for industries across Malaysia and Thailand.

Over the years, we are involved in producing and supplying high-quality industrial aerosol sprays, industrial specialty lubricants, greases & maintenance chemicals to our customers at competitive prices. With complete technical support from both overseas principles & in-house R&D department, we are also capable of customizing products accordingly to the respective customers' requirements.

Protean 3H1 Grease

Food Grade NSF 3H & H1 Direct Food Contact

Product Description

Protean 3H1 Grease is a specialized food grade grease with both NSF 3H and H1 registrations, making it suitable for both direct food contact and incidental food contact applications. This versatile grease provides excellent lubrication while meeting the strictest food safety requirements.

Key Features

  • NSF 3H registered for direct food contact
  • NSF H1 registered for incidental food contact
  • Excellent release properties
  • Superior water resistance
  • Clean, tasteless, and odorless
  • FDA 21 CFR 178.3570 compliant

Applications

  • Food processing equipment
  • Release applications
  • Bakery equipment
  • Conveyor systems
  • Packaging machinery
  • Meat and poultry processing

Benefits

  • Safe for direct food contact
  • Reduced contamination risk
  • Extended equipment life
  • Improved food safety compliance
  • Versatile applications

Protean Classic 2 (Poly Alpha Olefin)

NSF H1 Food-Safe NSF H1 Synthetic

Product Description

Protean Classic 2 is a high-performance synthetic lubricant based on Poly Alpha Olefin technology. Specifically formulated for food processing applications where extreme temperatures and demanding conditions exist. NSF H1 registered and suitable for use where incidental food contact may occur.

Key Features

  • NSF H1 registered for food processing applications
  • Outstanding thermal stability
  • Excellent low temperature performance
  • Superior oxidation resistance
  • Extended service life
  • Excellent wear protection

Applications

  • High-temperature food processing equipment
  • Cold storage applications
  • Bearings operating under extreme conditions
  • Food-safe chains and conveyors
  • Heavily loaded gearboxes

Benefits

  • Reduced maintenance costs
  • Lower lubricant consumption
  • Improved equipment reliability
  • Wide temperature range operation
  • Enhanced equipment protection

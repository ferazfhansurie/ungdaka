Protean Chain & Gear Oils

NSF H1 Food-Safe Multi-Purpose

Product Description

Protean Chain & Gear Oils are specially formulated NSF H1 lubricants designed for chains, conveyors, and gear systems in food processing equipment. These oils provide excellent wear protection and corrosion resistance while meeting food-safe requirements for incidental food contact.

Key Features

  • NSF H1 registered for food processing applications
  • Excellent wear and corrosion protection
  • Superior penetrating ability
  • Good adhesion to metal surfaces
  • Resistant to water washout
  • Clean, non-staining formulation

Applications

  • Food processing chains and conveyors
  • Enclosed gear systems
  • Drive chains
  • Packaging equipment
  • Bottling line machinery

Available Grades

  • ISO VG 150
  • ISO VG 220
  • ISO VG 320
  • ISO VG 460
